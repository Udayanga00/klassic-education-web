Important Study & Career Information

Visa Requirements:

X1 or X2 Visa (for long-term study) from the Chinese embassy.

Scholarships:

Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC) is highly sought after and covers fees/stipends.

Work while studying:

Formal part-time work is generally restricted, but internships may be permitted with university approval.

Post Study Work:

Limited. Graduates often need to secure an Employment Permit (Z-Visa) before leaving.
